STEAM CHALLENGE — NAWIS INNOVATION EVENT
The NAWIS STEAM MOE Challenge is a major school-hosted event focused on Science, Technology, Engineering, Arts, and Mathematics, aligned with the UAE/Saudi Ministry of Education innovation mandate.

STEAM EVENT OBJECTIVES:
1. Foster innovation, creativity, and problem-solving among students
2. Integrate AI, robotics, and digital tools into student projects
3. Encourage interdisciplinary thinking across STEM and arts
4. Develop 21st-century skills: collaboration, communication, critical thinking
5. Showcase student talent to parents, educators, and community stakeholders

STEAM CATEGORIES:
- Early Years (KG1–Grade 2): Creative art + simple science experiments
- Primary (Grade 3–5): Science models, coding basics, environmental projects
- Middle School (Grade 6–8): Robotics, AI prototypes, engineering challenges
- Secondary (Grade 9–12): Advanced AI projects, innovation showcases, entrepreneurship

STEAM TIMELINE:
- Project Registration: Students register their project ideas with their class teacher
- Mentoring Phase: Teachers guide students on project development (2-3 weeks)
- Internal School Showcase: Selected projects showcased within NAWIS
- Regional/MOE Submission: Top projects submitted to the Ministry of Education circuit
- Awards Ceremony: Prizes and certificates for outstanding projects

STEAM FOCUS AREAS FOR THIS YEAR:
- Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning applications
- Sustainable technology and green innovation
- Smart city solutions for Saudi Arabia's Vision 2030
- Health and wellness technology
- Educational technology tools

HOW TO PARTICIPATE IN STEAM:
Students interested in joining the STEAM challenge should speak to their class teacher or visit the school office. All grades are welcome to participate. Parent support and involvement is encouraged.
