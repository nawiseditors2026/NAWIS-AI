CBSE CURRICULUM:
NAWIS follows the Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE) curriculum from India, which is internationally recognized. The curriculum covers all core subjects with regular internal and board examinations. Grade 10 and Grade 12 students appear for CBSE board exams.

GRADE STRUCTURE:
- Pre-School (LKG and UKG)
- Primary (Grades 1-5)
- Middle School (Grades 6-8)
- Secondary (Grades 9-10) - CBSE Board Exams at Grade 10
- Senior Secondary (Grades 11-12) - Science or Commerce stream, CBSE Board Exams at Grade 12

EXAMINATION AND RESULTS:
Regular unit tests, mid-term, and final examinations are conducted as per the CBSE calendar. Results are shared with parents through the school portal. For result queries, please contact the academic office.

FEES AND FINANCE:
For fee structure and payment information, please contact the school administration office directly. Fee details are confidential and handled on an individual basis.

UNIFORM:
Students are required to wear the NAWIS school uniform on all school days. Uniform guidelines are provided during admission and available from the school office.

PARENT-TEACHER MEETINGS:
PTMs (Parent-Teacher Meetings) are scheduled periodically throughout the academic year. Parents are notified in advance through circulars and the school app.

HEALTH AND SAFETY:
NAWIS has a qualified nurse on campus during school hours. In case of medical emergencies, parents are contacted immediately. Students with specific medical conditions should inform the school administration.

EXTRA-CURRICULAR ACTIVITIES:
NAWIS encourages holistic development through various clubs and activities including Gavel Club (public speaking), Visual Performance Arts, Scouts and Guides, Model United Nations, B.E.S.T Sports Programme, Robotics and VR Lab, academic Olympiads, and the NAWIS Podcast.

HOLIDAYS:
The school follows the Saudi Arabia national holiday calendar in addition to CBSE academic calendar. The annual school calendar is shared at the beginning of each academic year.
