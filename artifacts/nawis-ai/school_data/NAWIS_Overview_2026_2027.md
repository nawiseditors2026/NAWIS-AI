## Curriculum (CBSE)
NAWIS follows the Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE) curriculum, one of India's most rigorous and internationally recognised boards. The curriculum covers all core subjects with regular internal and board examinations. Grade 10 and Grade 12 students appear for CBSE board exams. Senior Secondary offers two streams:
- **Science Stream:** Physics, Chemistry, Biology/Mathematics, English, Arabic/Computer Science
- **Commerce Stream:** Accountancy, Business Studies, Economics, English, Arabic/Maths
