Curriculum

NAWIS follows the Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE) curriculum — one of India's most rigorous and internationally respected educational boards. Grades 9–10 prepare students for the Grade 10 board examination, while Grades 11–12 offer Science and Commerce streams leading to the Grade 12 board exams.
